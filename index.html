The one stop San Francisco App that streamlines the process of donating items to charities

Help Your Neighbors

phone_android

Assisting others who need support in the community is the the sole vision of Kifu. Watch your contributions make a difference in San Francisco by simply creating a posting or a delivery!

Donators:

See local donation listings where donators have all of their listed items. Your postings will be displayed so that volunteers can request to do the delivery for you.

Volunteers:

Setting up a volunteer delivery is as easy as finding the listings that are closest to you. Request to deliver a donation by tapping the post of your choosing. Let the app handle all your delivery coordination

Safety For All:

Your dropoff locations will never be exposed until a donator accepts a delivery request from a person of their choosing. Accept the volunteers you trust by checking their contribution points and reputation on the app.

Volunteer Hours:

Collect contribution points by donating items or volunteering to deliver the donated items. Compete against your friends while helping the community of San Francisco!
